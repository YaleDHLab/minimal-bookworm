VIII. A Note Communicated by Mr. Hisl, confirming the great Age of Henry Jenkins, mentioned No 221. p. 265. of these Transactions. IN the Kings Remembrancers Office in the Exchequer, is a record of a deposition in a cause by English Bill, between Ant. Clark and Smirkson, taken April 1665, at Kettering in Yorkshire, where Henry Jenkins of Ellerton upon Swale Labourer Aged 157 Years was produced and deposed as a Witness.
