Dr Plukenet makes this a Cyperus grass, but for what reason I know not, having as yet seen nothing but its Leaves. This is a most delicate sort of Fragrant Grass,which being rubb'd, smells like Baume and Lime or Limon-peel together. The Portuguese Women fume their Children with it, and give the Decoction of it with other things for Fevers, and to strengthen weak Stomachs; but the Natives use it not, which together, with its growing in Gardens on the Sea Coast, and not up the Country, as I can yet observe, makes me think the Portuguese brought this from other parts and planted it here; certainly so excellenta Plant of such Fragrant and Aromatick taste must have many Vertues. I use it in many cases, and generally with success.
