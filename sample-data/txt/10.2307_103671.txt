Advertisement. WHereas I publish'd a Paper concerning the Figure of the Earth, in the Philosophical Transactions, Numb. 388, and a Paragraph which I cancell'd, was, by the mistake of my Amanuensis, printed whilst I was sick: I desire such Readers as have that Transaction by them, to cancel from the Words, Lastly, Let us suppose, Page 280. Lin. ult. to the End of the Paragraph. Having from time to time neglected to give this notice, I chose to do it here, tho'late, rather than wholly to omit it. J. T. Desaguliers.
