AN ADVERTISEMENT. All persons that have the Volums of these Transactions intire, are desired to add a stroke in the title page of the preceding one to this, and make it the XII Volum; Mr. Oldenburgh having publisht 4 Transactions after his Eleventh Volum, to which, the six, set forth by Dr Grew, being added, as indeed they ought, for that they carry on the old number; they may well pass for the XII Volume: so that the two last, for the years 1683 and 1684 must be accounted the XIII and XIV Volums.
