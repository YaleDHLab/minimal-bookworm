The thermometer A was within the lower observatory, on the south-east side, close to the barometer; and its ball was exactly the same height with the mercury in the bason thereof. The thermometer marked B was without doors, on the north side of the observatory. The signs + and which are annexed to the altitudes of the two thermometers signify, that they stood so many degrees above or below the cypher respectively. Both the thermometers are according to Fahrenheit's scale. W. W. XV. An
