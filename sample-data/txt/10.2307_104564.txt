[409] VI. A Roman Inscription found at Bath, communicated to the Royal Society by the Rev. William Stukely M. D. Fellow of the Coll. of Phys. F. R. S. and Rector of St. George the Martyr, London. L ~ VITELLIVS ~ MAXIMI NI~I ~ F ~ T ~ ANCINVS ~ CIVES ~ HISP ~ CAVRIESIS EQ ~ ALAE VETTONVM ~ CR ANN ~ XXXXVI ~ STIP ~ XXVI ~ H ~ S ~ ~ ~ Thus to be read. Lucius Vitellius Maximiniani filius Titus Ancinus, * civis Hispanus Cauriensis ~ equitum alae Vettonum curator, annos XLVI. stipendii XXVI. hic sepultus est. Read June 30. 1748. VII.
