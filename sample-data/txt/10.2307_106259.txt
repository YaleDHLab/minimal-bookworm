But the Abbe ROCHON has only made use of the double refraction of the rock crystal for his micrometer, and I am assured he has said, that his prism could give him no more than six degrees. Now it is well known, that pieces of rock crystal, large enough and pure enough for these purposes, are extremely rare; besides, the difficulty of working them is great, that substance being harder than glass, and requiring the utmost attention in cutting, in order to obtain the difference desired between the two refractions.
