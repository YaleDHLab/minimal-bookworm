A D V E R T I S E M E N T. Whereas the small Numbers of Royal-Paper Books usually Printed, have occasion'd great Complaints from several curious Men, and sometimes the great Numbers of such Books have made considerable Losses in the way of Trade: To remedy such Uncertainties for the future in all Impressions of Books, as shall be Printed at the Theater in Oxford, it is desired, that those Gentlemen, who are willing to have Books of the larger Paper, fignifie their Names to some Person known to John Hall, Ware-house Keeper there.
