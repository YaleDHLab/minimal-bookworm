A Letter from Benjamin Franklin, LL. D. and F. R. S. to the Reverend Thomas Birch, D. D. and Secretary to the Royal Society. S I R, Craven-Street, Feb. 4, 1762. MR. Canton did me the favour to shew me the ingenious experiments he has described in the inclosed letter. They succeeded perfectly as he has related them; and I imagine, the communication of them must be agreeable to the curious in this branch of natural knowledge. Read Feb. 4, 1762. I am, S I R, With great respect, Your most obedient and most humble servant, B. Franklin. LXXI. A
