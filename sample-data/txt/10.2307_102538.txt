XII. A way of Guilding Gold upon Silver. Communicated by the same. BEat a Ducket thin, and dissolve it in Two Ounces of Aqua Regia, then dip therein a clean Rag, and let the same dry, and do it again and again till all the said Liquor be soak'd up, then burn the said Rag, and with the Tinder thereof let Silver be rub'd, using therewith a little Spittle, and if by chance the Silver will not take, then hold it to the Fire to take away all manner of Grease and it will not fail.
