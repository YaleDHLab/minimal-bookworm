VI. An Observation of the same Eclipse of the Sun, by Mr. J. Milner, at Yeovil in Somersetshire, communicated in a Letter to Dr. John Allen, M. D. F. R. S. S I R, I Beg leave to present you with the Observations I made on the Eclipse May 2, viz. The Beginning at 5.h 34.m 00s 17.o 45~ at 6. 00. 00 13. 36 Sun's Alt. End at 7. 14. 30 2. 45 I made Use of a Quadrant 2 Feet radius. J. Milner. Lat. Yeovil, 51~. May 4, 1733.
