E R R A T A. P. 240. lin. penult. for P Q R U, the path of the tail broke off over the trees, read Q R S U the path. E F the tail broke off. In the plate, E F the breaking of the tail, should not have been represented as directly over the trees, but a little more to the southward, so as to bring the ball W (viz. the meteor at its extinction) perpendicularly over the said trees.
