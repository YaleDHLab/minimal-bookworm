(68) III. An Extraordinary high Tide in the River Thames observ'd by Capt. T H O. J O N E S. M A R C H the 8th, 1725-6, The Tide in the River Thames, at New Crane in Shadwell, flow'd twenty Foot, five Inches and a half, taken by a Level, from that High-water Mark, to Low-water the next Morning, and was four Inches higher than has been known these fourty Years.
